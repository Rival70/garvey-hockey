Teams

Lisnagarvey host ten senior teams, five youth teams and eight mini teams. Each category is measured by the players age and overall skill level which ultimately leads better players who are in senior for example, would be in M1 or W1. Mens 1 and Womens 1 are listed here as these are the main teams that represent Lisnagarvey and have the pleasure of representing Northern Ireland and Ireland in general.
